# Exercise 04: Demonstrate Knowledge Management features to a customer


Now that you've configured the feature to meet your needs, you're going to demo the functionality to the customer. Typically, the knowledge management piece will be part of a bigger case management and case resolution demonstration. Below you're highlighted multiple components of the demonstration.

## Talk track

Imagine a customer in Brazil has contacted you about their machine which is no longer brewing coffee as intended. After some self-service trouble shooting the case is escalated to a representative who is going to assist the customer in troubleshooting and resolving their issue.

### Business value statements:

These represent themes that should be part of your narrative. As you demo the functionality to your customer you should come back to these themes and reiterate them throughout you demo.

- Improve customer satisfaction with faster, more accurate responses.
- Streamline case resolution with intelligent case management and AI assistance.
- Simplify knowledge transfer with intelligent article creation.

### Items to highlight as you're demonstrating capabilities:

- Emphasize how **Copilot** reduces agent effort with real-time suggestions.
- Highlight how **Case Management Agent** automates case creation and classification.
- Show how **Knowledge Management** turns resolved cases into reusable content.
- Demonstrate **multi-session handling** for productivity.
